What you get

Every signal that matters to a recruiter

HireMind AI doesn't give you a vague percentage. It breaks down every dimension an ATS system evaluates, so you know exactly what to fix.

ATS Score (0–100)

A weighted composite: 60% keyword match, 20% sections, 10% length, 10% format. Mirrors how real ATS systems rank candidates.

Matched & Missing Keywords

See exactly which keywords from the job description appear in your resume, and which ones are absent — with up to 40 terms per list.

Section Completeness

Checks for the presence of Summary, Experience, Education, Skills, Projects, and Certifications — the six sections recruiters expect.

Category Prediction

Our ANN model predicts which of 24 job categories your resume targets — with confidence score and top-3 matches, so you can pivot if needed.

Format Signals

Detects bullet points, quantified achievements, action verbs, and date formatting — the four format signals that most affect readability and ATS parse success.

Domain Skill Gaps

Beyond the job description, we compare your resume against curated skill sets for your predicted category — surfacing domain keywords you may have forgotten to include.
